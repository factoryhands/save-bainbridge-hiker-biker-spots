SAVE BIKE CAMPING

at FAY BAINBRIDGE PARK

Sometime before the summer camping season of 2023 Fay Bainbridge Park quietly reclassified their hiker/biker and kayaker campsites as non-reservable sites open to motorists. This is a huge blow to the cycling and kayaking community in Seattlea and the surrounding area. Gone are the days where you could be guaranteed a spot at Fay Bainbridge for arriving under your own steam. Let's save ths spots!
